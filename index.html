About Me

"Hello world! I am Alex Balaoro, a former police officer turned web developer, blogger, and everything in between"

Welcome to my blog, a Bicolano’s journey through travel, food, and everything in between! From my roots in the vibrant Bicol region, where every dish packs a punch and each view leaves you breathless, I bring a unique blend of adventure and storytelling as a former police officer and now web developer.

Join me as I uncover hidden destinations, savor local and exotic flavors, and share tips for making the most of every journey. Whether it’s spicy Bicol Express or tales from the road, this blog captures it all—one experience, one story, one flavor at a time!

Popular Destinations

Discover the most breathtaking locations in Albay, from volcanic landscapes to historical sites

Mayon Volcano

Known for its perfect cone shape, Mayon Volcano is an active stratovolcano and the province's iconic landmark.

Learn More

Cagsawa Ruins

A testament to Mayon's destructive power, these ruins tell the story of the 1814 eruption that buried the town of Cagsawa.

Learn More

Sumlang Lake

A serene lake offering stunning views of Mayon Volcano, perfect for bamboo rafting and photography.
